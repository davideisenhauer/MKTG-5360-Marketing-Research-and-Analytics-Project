Relationship test

We used a chi square for our relationship test. We conducted a chi square test of independence to examine the relationship between familiarity between Habitat for Humanity and motivation to shop at the ReStore when purchases support Habitat for Humanity’s mission.

Hypothesis:
H₀: No relationship between familiarity and motivation
H₁: Relationship exists

The chi square test produced a test statistic of:
X^2 = ~4.8; df = 1

Since this value exceeds the critical value of 3.84 (α = 0.05), the null hypothesis is rejected

Findings:
There is a statistically significant relationship between familiarity with Habitat for Humanity and motivation to shop at the ReStore. Individuals who are familiar with the organization are more likely to be motivated by its mission when making decisions on where to shop.
